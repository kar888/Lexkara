THE FIRM

About Lexkara & Co

The Firm

Lexkara & Co is a strategic legal consultancy operating on a chambers-style model: lean, senior, and directly accessible. The firm was founded by Kenneth A. to address a structural gap in the UK legal market — the absence of a credible alternative between the overhead-laden Magic Circle model and the limitations of conventional high-street practice. Lexkara & Co exists for clients who demand Magic Circle-level diligence and analysis but value the agility, responsiveness, and direct partner engagement that a specialist adviser and curated senior network can provide.

The firm provides strategic legal support to executives, boards, companies, and investors across the full spectrum of corporate and commercial law. Unlike traditional solicitors' firms, Lexkara & Co does not operate with layers of associates and trainees. Kenneth A. is personally and directly available on every matter — from the initial strategy call to the final negotiation. There is no junior hand-off, and there is no waiting list.

This model reflects a conviction that the most valuable thing a legal adviser can offer is not volume capacity but judgment — the judgment that comes from twenty-one years of practice across corporate transactions, commercial disputes, insolvency, regulatory matters, and cross-border work. Clients of Lexkara & Co receive that judgment from the first conversation, not after it has been filtered through a team hierarchy.

REGULATORY POSITION

Professional Standing

Lexkara & Co is a legal consultancy. The firm is not authorised or regulated by the Solicitors Regulation Authority (SRA) or the Bar Standards Board (BSB). Kenneth A. is a barrister called to the Bar of England and Wales at the Inner Temple in 2005, and he provides legal services through Lexkara & Co in his capacity as a legal consultant.

The firm provides non-reserved legal advice and legal strategy. This encompasses the full range of advisory legal work — including corporate advice, commercial negotiations, regulatory guidance, and strategic counsel — that does not constitute "reserved legal activities" as defined by the Legal Services Act 2007. Reserved legal activities (such as the conduct of litigation, rights of audience in court, and the administration of oaths) are, where required, carried out by panel solicitors and barristers who hold their own authorisation and are regulated by the SRA or BSB respectively.

Lexkara & Co does not hold client funds. All client monies related to transactions or disputes are held by regulated third parties in accordance with their own professional obligations.

This regulatory model is an intentional design choice. It allows Lexkara & Co to deliver senior-level legal counsel with the flexibility, efficiency, and direct-access model that clients value — without the structural constraints and cost overheads of traditional regulated law firm structures. The quality of the legal advice is assured by Kenneth A.'s professional qualifications, his twenty-one years of experience, and his ongoing obligations as a member of the Bar.
